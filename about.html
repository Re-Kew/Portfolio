After an exciting career in sound design, music production and recording studio technology, I made a switch to visual design in 2018 and graduated with the highest grade as an interaction designer at the School of Design, Bern.
Getting to the bottom of problems, focusing on the needs of the target group and working out solutions for them - this is the principle I acquired and internalized during my studies to become an interaction designer. Furthermore, my work focuses strongly on aesthetics, identity and a user interface that is smooth and pleasant to use.
What I like: Typography, making best practices, creating animations, designing weird & catchy sounds, organizing things, creating structure, contemporary art, freehand drawing, recharging one's batteries in nature, cuddling dogs and cats, and working with upbeat, driven people who are good at what they do. And importantly, not to miss out on the fun!
